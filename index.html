Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 1, 2026

Nova Applications ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Nova Adventures (also referred to as MicroAdventures) mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum amount of personal data required to provide you with a secure and functional application experience.

  • Account Information: When you register an account, we collect your email address and account password (which is securely hashed on our servers).
  • App Data: Any user-generated content or completions created within the app are stored securely on our database.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect strictly for administrative and security purposes, including:

  • Creating and managing your user account.
  • Sending automatic account verification emails upon signup.
  • Sending security alerts and notifications to your email address whenever a new sign-in is detected.
  • Maintaining the performance and security of our backend infrastructure.

Data Storage and Third-Party Services

Your privacy and data security are highly important to us. We handle your data using the following infrastructure:

  • Self-Hosted Infrastructure: Your account data and app content are securely stored on our private database instance (powered by PocketBase). Network traffic to our database is routed securely through encrypted tunnels via Cloudflare.
  • Email Delivery: We utilize Brevo, a third-party transactional email delivery service, to securely transmit verification links and account sign-in alerts to your inbox. Brevo does not use your email address for any marketing purposes.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with third-party advertisers or external data brokers.

Security of Your Information

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data, including end-to-end encryption for data in transit and cryptographic password hashing. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Data Rights

You retain full control over your data. At any time, you can:

  • Request access to the data stored about your account.
  • Request the complete erasure of your account and all associated application data.
